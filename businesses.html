The Arc of Atlantic County

About The Arc Makes Cents

The Arc of Atlantic County operates two thrift shops: "The Arc Makes Cents" in Ventnor and "The Arc Makes Cents, Too" in Egg Harbor Township. These stores promote sustainable shopping by selling donated, gently used clothing and accessories.

Proceeds from the shops support programs for individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities while reducing textile waste. Visit their website for more information.

American Honey Ladies Resale Boutique

About American Honey Ladies Resale Boutique

Located in Galloway Township, American Honey Ladies Resale offers fashionable clothing at 50-75% off the brand price. The boutique provides a curated shopping experience sorting by size and color for convenience.

Beyond clothing, they feature locally made merchandise, including candles, lip balms, and honey jars, supporting small, woman-owned businesses. Regular customers enjoy perks like punch cards and discounts for military personnel.

Learn more on their Facebook page.

Second Chance Thrift Shop

About Second Chance Thrift Shop

Located in Egg Harbor Township, NJ, Second Chance Thrift Shop offers a wide range of gently used clothing, accessories, and household items. The shop promotes sustainable shopping by reducing textile waste and extending the life cycle of pre-loved items.

Proceeds from the store support local charitable initiatives, creating a positive impact on the community. Shop, donate, or explore their mission to make a difference.

Learn more on their Facebook page.

The Days Sustainable Apparel

About The Days Sustainable Apparel

Located in Cape May, NJ, The Days Sustainable Apparel offers clothing made from upcycled and plant-based materials. Their collection is hand-designed locally in New Jersey and ethically produced in the USA.

This boutique emphasizes slow fashion, featuring timeless designs that minimize environmental impact. Their mission is to create durable, high-quality pieces that promote sustainable shopping habits.

Learn more about their eco-friendly clothing by visiting their page.
